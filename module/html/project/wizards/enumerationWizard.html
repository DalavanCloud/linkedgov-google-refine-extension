Enumeration wizard

The enumeration wizard is used to expand any cryptic codes/symbols within the data.

These can range from simple compass directions (N, S, E, W) to government department acronyms (DfT, HMRC, DEFRA)

This wizard asks you to:

  1. Select a column containing any codes/symbols
  2. Expand them so they are readable by typing into their text-boxes
  3. Try to complete the group by adding any other missing codes/symbols
  4. Click "Update"
Pick column To select multiple column headings, hold down CTRL (Windows) or Apple (Mac). You can also drag your mouse across multiple column headers while holding down the relevant key.

Click anywhere in the table to unselect the columns.
